Deadline to Apply for Federal Financial Aid

 

The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (the FAFSA) opens each fall. Fill out your FAFSA after you apply to find out if you qualify for federal financial aid.

 

March 1 is the priority filing deadline, but you can submit your FAFSA application even earlier. The sooner your FAFSA application is submitted, the better your chances are to qualify for free grant money and federal loan eligibility – and, the sooner you’ll hear back from FAFSA about the amount of aid you qualify for. 

 

University of Arizona FAFSA school code: 001083

Merit Scholarship Deadlines

You may also qualify for a merit-based scholarship based on your academic performance. Each merit scholarship has its own eligibility requirements and deadlines. 

 

To qualify for merit-based scholarship consideration, you must submit your complete application for admission by May 3, 2021.

Paying Tuition

Tuition is paid through the University of Arizona Bursar’s Office. After you’re admitted and have enrolled, you can access your account balance through UAccess. Visit the Bursar's website for specific tuition dates, deadlines, the last day to receive a refund, and more.
