We’re Ready to Help

You’re trying to apply to and enroll at the University of Arizona. We’re here to help. Contact the Office of Admissions to learn more about transitioning to the University of Arizona, or connect with your recruiter to learn more about life as a Wildcat.

 

All admissions team members are currently working remotely. The University of Arizona Office of Admissions is closed to visitors; however, we are available during regular business hours of 8am–5pm (MST) to respond to your messages.
