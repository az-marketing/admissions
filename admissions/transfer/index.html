Complete Your Education

Take your hard work to the next level. If you have 12 or more transferable college credits – earned post-high school, at a community college, or at another university – the University of Arizona considers you a transfer student.

 

Pathways to Transfer

We have many pathways for transfer students at Arizona – the main campus in Tucson is our hub, but online and distance locations (our Near You Network) offer flexibility. No matter which pathway you choose, your Arizona degree will set you up for success.
